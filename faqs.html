Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Lift International. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

General

What does Lift International do?

Lift International is a premium crane and heavy equipment company offering rentals, sales, and rent-to-own programs. We also provide certified crane operators and deliver equipment to project sites across South America, Africa, the Caribbean, and beyond.

Where do you operate?

We serve projects in over 15 countries, primarily across South America (Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Argentina, Chile) and Africa (Nigeria, Angola, South Africa, Ghana, Kenya). We also handle projects in the Caribbean and other regions on a case-by-case basis. Our head office is located in Burlington, Ontario, Canada.

How long has Lift International been in business?

With over 20 years of experience in the heavy lifting industry, we've completed more than 500 projects worldwide. Our team brings deep expertise in crane logistics, rigging, and project management across diverse environments and industries.

Crane Rentals

What types of cranes do you rent?

We offer a full range of lifting equipment including mobile cranes (5t–500t), crawler cranes (100t–3,500t), all-terrain cranes (50t–1,200t), tower cranes, telehandlers, and forklifts. If you need a specific machine, we can source it globally.

What is the difference between bare rental and operated rental?

Bare rental provides the crane only — your team supplies the operator and rigging crew. Operated rental includes the crane plus one or more of our certified operators. Most international clients choose operated rental for convenience and compliance with local safety regulations.

What rental terms are available?

We offer flexible daily, weekly, and monthly rental terms. For long-term projects (6+ months), we provide discounted rates and can structure custom agreements. All contracts include maintenance support and 24/7 emergency assistance.

How quickly can I get a quote?

We aim to deliver a detailed, competitive quote within one hour during business hours (Mon–Fri 8am–5pm, Sat 8am–2pm Eastern). Simply fill out our quote request form or call us directly.

Sales & Rent-to-Own

Do you sell new and used cranes?

Yes. We maintain an inventory of both new and certified used equipment. Every used crane comes with full documentation, maintenance records, inspection certificates, and photos. We can also source specific models globally if it's not in our current stock.

How does Rent-to-Own work?

Our Rent-to-Own program lets you start with a standard rental and apply a portion of each payment toward the purchase price. There's no large upfront capital requirement. At the end of the agreed term, you own the equipment outright. It's ideal for growing contractors who need equipment now but want to spread the cost over time.

Can you arrange international delivery for purchased equipment?

Absolutely. We handle all logistics including transport, customs documentation, and on-site delivery. Whether you're shipping to a port in Lagos or a construction site in Bogotá, we coordinate the entire process so you can focus on your project.

Operators & Safety

Are your operators certified?

Yes. All Lift International operators hold valid certifications (NCCCO, CPCS, or equivalent local credentials) and undergo regular safety training. We match operators to the specific crane type and project environment to ensure maximum safety and efficiency.

What safety standards do you follow?

We adhere to OSHA, ANSI, ISO, and local regulatory standards for every project. All equipment undergoes regular third-party inspections, and we maintain detailed maintenance logs. Learn more on our Safety Standards page.

Pricing & Payment

How do your prices compare to competitors?

Our rates consistently beat bank financing and larger competitors. Because we operate with lower overhead and source equipment globally, we pass those savings on to our clients without cutting corners on quality, maintenance, or service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, certified checks, and major credit cards. For long-term contracts, we can arrange custom payment schedules. All pricing is provided in USD, though we can invoice in local currencies where applicable.
